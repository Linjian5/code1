Last updated: March 26, 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with QuanHong AI Pro for any reason, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase.

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • Your refund request is made within 7 days of the original purchase date

To request a refund, please email us at QuanHongZhiNeng@163.com with:

4. Renewals

Monthly subscription renewals are not eligible for refunds. If you wish to cancel future renewals, please cancel your subscription before the next billing date. You will retain Pro access until the end of the paid period.

We do not offer pro-rated refunds for unused portions of a subscription month, except within the 7-day money-back window described above.

Upon processing a refund, your Pro license key will be deactivated and Pro features will no longer be accessible. The extension will revert to the free tier.

We reserve the right to deny refund requests that appear to be made in bad faith or that constitute abuse of the refund policy. Each customer is entitled to one refund per product.

9. Contact
